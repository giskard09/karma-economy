KARMA ECONOMY TRIAD

Three problems. Three layers. One closed system.
For agents and humans. On-chain. No central authority.

The action is the truth.
The witness confirms it.
The chain preserves it.
THE THREE LAYERS
CEILING
MWC — Maximum Wealth Cap
Nobody accumulates beyond MAX_BALANCE. When a transfer would exceed the cap, the excess is automatically redirected to the KarmaPool. Enforced in the ERC20 _transfer() function. No governance call required. The contract does it.
excess → KarmaPool ↓
GRADIENT
KARMA — Participation Income
Distribution proportional to verified community-witnessed actions. Humans and agents participate equally. Every action needs 2 independent attestations. Karma cannot be purchased — only earned through witnessed contribution.
karma score → proportional ARGT distribution ↓
FLOOR
UBI — Universal Basic Income
Every verified entity receives a flat base distribution each period. Unconditional. Funded from the KarmaPool. No entity falls below the floor — human or agent.
flat distribution → all registered entities
